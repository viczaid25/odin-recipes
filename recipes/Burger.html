Butter Burger

A delicious burger with caramelized onion

    Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ⅔ pound 90% lean ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning (such as Trader Joe's® 21 Seasoning Salute)
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 slices white Cheddar cheese (such as Sartori®)
  • 2 (2 ounce) hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter (such as Plugra®)
    Steps
  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat, then add olive oil. Allow butter to brown. Reduce heat to low and add onion. Cover and cook over very low heat, stirring occasionally, until very soft, about 20 minutes. You do not want to caramelize the onion.
  2. Meanwhile, season ground beef with Italian seasoning and shape into 2 hamburger patties.
  3. Melt ghee in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook hamburger patties, about 3 minutes per side. Add cheese onto each patty and allow to melt.
  4. Meanwhile, preheat the oven's broiler and toast buns until crisp, 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Set hamburger patties on the bottom buns and add add sauteed onions on top. Spread 1 tablespoon butter across the freshly toasted top bun.
